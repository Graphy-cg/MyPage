Luis Hernán Herrera Becerra

About me

I am an Instructor Professor at Universidad Tecnológica Metropolitana (UTEM). In 2023, I obtained my PhD in Informatics and Computer Science at Universidad de Santiago de Chile, and I was fortunate to be advised by PhD Pablo Pérez-Lantero and PhD Carlos Seara. Prior to that, I graduated as a Bachelor in Computer Science at Universidad de Santiago de Chile. My research interests lie in the intersection of Computational Geometry and Discrete Mathematics. I am also interested in Intersection Graph Theory and Geometric Algorithms. Some specific topics I work on include Special kind of Hulls of point sets, Geometric Intersection Graphs, and Combinatorial Problems over Bicolored point sets.
